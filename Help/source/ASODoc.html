Advanced Skyrim Overhaul (ASO) v4.5

Short description

This mod is a continuation of Nature of the beast mod. This mod add many sophisticated and original spells, abilities and mechanics that will revolutionize your gameplay.

There are 3 major parts of the mod that however are interconnected with one another:

Beast part


This part adds several biological abilities and is best for werewolf players or werebeast players to be precise (for there are several werebeast forms available). It should also suit players that play as other beast races both vanilla (vampires, argonians, khajiits) and custom. The purpose of this part of the mod is to give the player the feeling he/she is really playing as a beast and even to command a horde of other beasts, to empower the player and to let him/her experience the Skyrim as never before. Dont let the above fool you. You can add any lesser power no matter what race you are playing.

In practice you can gain:
  • an automatic and immersive night eye that gradually turns on and off night eye effect depending on the amount of light on the player,
  • a realistic smell that depends on the strength and direction of wind and allows you to track targets/plants/ingredients/food(even when in containers)/water/fire/etc from significant distance while providing you with many informations about them. Be advise though, you can be smelled as well even while not having the smell lesser powers,
  • adrenaline surge that makes you faster (2 versions: turbo or slow-motion), stronger, increases jump height and reduces fall damage at the cost of stamina,
  • 6th sense that warns you when you are about to get hit either in melee, by spell or arrow and can trigger the above adrenaline surge if you choose so (if you choose slow-motion version of adrenaline surge you have a good chance to dodge the attack),
  • realistic heat vision that takes into account events like death, swimming and rain (not under roof - roof detection!) and gradually turns on or off visual effects on nearby targets (when swimming the change is instant). Be advise though, some actors you can sense your heat as well even while you dont have the heat sense lesser powers
  • lesser power allowing you to transform into alpha werewolf and back at will,
  • chance to live as an Alpha Werewolf indefinatelly, to eat, drink and sleep in this form,
  • chance to amass a wolf/werewolf pack consisting of wolves and werewolves you meet while being in Alpha (or vanilla) Werewolf form and command the pack by issuing simple commands like wait, follow me, wait there and forward (and with Moonlight Tales much more),
  • chance to live as a Green Bane (giant monstrous yet stealthy lizard) indefinatelly, to eat, drink and sleep in this form, the lizard has its own unique abilties like camouflage (partial invisibility), spit, burrow and poisoned claws,
  • chance to live as a mighty Werebear indefinatelly, to eat, drink and sleep in this form;
  • chance to live as a flying, winged dragorian and to hunt in this form like eagles do;
  • chance to climb trees/buildings, sneak in the beast form;
  • chance to meet a green bane and learn the true meaning of terror or to meet your best pet ever.


Minion system part

minion system is a system that allows the player to control different kind of actors such as followers/hirelings/follower pets/ summoned or reanimated creatures (minions). It is perfect for any player that wants to play as a commander / necromancer / summoner / constructor commanding different kind of actors

In practice you can gain:
  • ability to command followers/hirelings/follower dogs/summmons without fighting yourself (wait, wait there, follow, forward, attack commands via custom buttons);
  • ability to make your more advanced minions a labor force that harvest, mine, process and sell resources (including crafting and upgrading items, cooking, making camp etc)
  • new necromantic spells that raise/summon undead or create soul reavers - harvesters of souls
  • summoning spell to summon your minions from afar
  • portal spell that allows you to send minions to different places

Smell

  • Automatically added to player if he has a "Night Eyes" lesser power or waterbreathing constant effect.
Rewritten Marker
  • If you press "]" button you activate a smell scan of nearby area. The key can be customised in the ring menu. You can also actiate the ability by choosing appropriate power in magic menu and pressing the power button;
  • The ability shows direction and strenght of wind, direction you are currently heading (only in interiors) and detected NPCs/creatures/plants/ingreidents/food/water/fire/etc; 
  • If you hold the toggle button and then release it you activate/deactivate passive smell. It notifies you about random detected object every few sec. You can also turn it on or off by using appropriate lesser power. You can tweak PassiveSmellInterval (value 0-100) variable in the ring menu to determine the frequency of the ability;
  • The ability depends on the direction and strength of wind; 
  • The direction of the wind determines whether you can detect current object while being in exterior world space. For example when the wind is north it means that it blows from the north and that you can detect objects north of you. When no wind or in interior you can detect object no matter its position;
  • The strength of wind affects how distant objects player can smell. The stronger the father. Maxdistance in interior = 4000.0. Maxdistance in exterior = 4000+4000+WindStrength.GetValue()*16000.0. This means that potentially the greatest range is 24000, but in practice unless you change the number of grids and cells being loaded the maximum number is 16000. Be warned changing the number of grids and cells in "Skyrim.ini" file can cause crashes and other problems; 
  • The strength also affects the angle of detection (both vertical and horizontal). You can detect only objects that lie in the direction of wind. Max angle = 45 - (22*wind speed); 
  • The smell provides you with information about actor's name (only if you have learned its scent otherwise it will use word Known - for known non-unique actors or Unknown - for all unknown actors), race, sex, condition (whether its dead or alive, it informs about death if current actor died at least 6 hours ago) and intensity of the scent (in percent). I have decided to show intensity of scent because in my opinion there is no other realistic way. When using smell you can only determine whether the current scent is stronger or weaker. The stronger the scent the closer the source, and the weaker the scent the further the source. In addition the max range of smell is fluid and depends on the strength of wind. In other words, in order to determine distance to the source in meters or feets while using a smell you would have to in reality use some kind of calculator taking into account intensity of scent and strength of wind. As far as I know, there are no calculators in Skyrim. Smell detects if current NPC is a vampire. Smell detects if current NPC is a werewolf (works on vanilla werewolves and any custom that has werewolf immunity ability or werewolf change power, so you can detect your followers as werewolves while using Amazing Follower Tweaks). You can learn scents of actors by approaching them and aiming at them while passive smell is turned on but only if you can sense them (this a good way to mark actors as netralized). You can sense most kind of actors. You cannot sense ghosts or magic anomalies. You cannot use smell while swimming. You cannot smell actors that are swimming. When smelling other objects only name of the object and intensity is provided;
  • After the smell sequance you have the option to track a specific object detected by the smell (added visual effect: red, blue, green or strong orange on tracked object that shows you only a direction and not the object itself which I think is more realistic). If you activate the smell again or you are unable to track object anymore for some time you turn off the smell track mode (the visual effect is removed immediatelly). While in smell tracking mode passive smell provides you with information only about tracked object if you can sense it (if not information is provided as default). If you initiate smell while passive smell is active and you sensed some object you are given an option to scan the surrounding area (classic smell ability) or skip that and immediately start to track the recently detected object;
  • Some actors can smell you as well. This means once you are smelled deers/elks should run from you and aggressive, confident and hostile actors should come to you (sic!). While approaching them take into consideration the direction and strength of wind. Beware animals, argonians, khajjits, vampires and werewolves. They cannot detect you when you are swimming. If you enter a new interior world space you are initially almost impossible to detect, but it gradually changes with time when your scent spreads in new place. In other word at the beginning you have a natural advantage. It doesnt matter whether you have a smell ability. Even if you dont they can still sense you. You can turn on or off detection by some actors of the player by tweaking ActorSmellDetectionTurnedOn variable in the ring setup; 
  • Modders can choose to add to their actors the ability to smell the player. In order to do so the race must have the following vanilla keywords: LinkCustom06, LinkCustom08, LinkCustom10;
  • Be advised! The detection of player by actors requires a script to be run on any loaded actor and can potentially affect the speed of the game;
  • While using the smell abilities you have the option to track closest drinkable water (searches for the same objects as Use water lesser power from IMPs More complex Needs or Realistic Needs and Diseases). Smell will in the first place try to locate water from waterfalls (fresh, spring water) if RND mod is activated. Remember to stand still for few seconds;
  • You can detect fire while using smell. This fires are recognised by Frostfall as sources of heat;
  • You can detect other resources like food/potions/plants/ingredients/organic containers, if none detected - resources in containers, if you are in werebear form honey has absolute priority;
  • when using passive smell the detection goes as follows, first actors, if none detected - resources like food/potions/plants/ingredients/organic containers, if none detected - fire, if none detected - water, if none detected - resources in containers, if you are in werebear form honey has absolute priority
  • You can cook a Scent Camouflage Potion while having 1 blue, purple and red mountain flower. The potion will completly hide your scent for some time, then the efects will gradually fade.

Adrenaline surge

  • The mod will automatically add adrenaline surge ability to the player that has any night eye lesser power or waterbreathing constant effect;
  • Temporarily increases your speed, increases maximum carry weight, increases your jump (but not if AdrenalineSurgeJumpHeight is set to 0), reduces fall damage and increases several combat stats but constantly damages stamina; 
  • You can turn it off at any time. Automatically turns off when your stamina drops below 10;
  • If you press "-" button you turn on adrenaline surge. If you press "-" button again you turn off adrenaline surge. The key can be customised in the ring menu. You can also turn on or off the ability by choosing appropriate power in magic menu and pressing the power button;
  • Two versions:
    • time slows down,
    • time doesnt slow down but you move/attack faster;
  • You choose version by tweaking AdrenalineSurgeFast variable in ring settings;
  • In the same way you can also change the AdrenalineSurgeJumpHeight (value from 100 to 500);
  • As a rookie your physical condition is lower and you can use adrenaline surge for shorter period of time. When your level is below 21 you will receive additional damage to stamina. The lower your level the higher the damage; 
  • If you hold adrenaline surge button (Note: you dont have to have the adrenaline surge) you will launch the survival menu that will enable you to transform into werewolf form and back and to sleep anywhere, to use check wind, check needs status from IMPs More Complex Needs or Realistic Needs and Diseases and use weather sense from Frostfall, drink (use water from IMPs More Complex Needs or Realistic Needs and Diseases) and use ring menu to tweak mod settings while in werewolf form.
  • you cannot use adrenaline surge when flying as Dragorian

6th sense

  • The mod will automatically add 6th sense ability to the player that has any night eye lesser power or waterbreathing constant effect;
  • It warns you if someone or something wants to attack you and provides you with direction of the threat. In other words the ability triggers when an actor is targeting the player;
  • Two versions:
    • only notification (it is accompanied by sound effect),
    • notification+trigger of adrenaline surge (player must have adrenaline surge, adrenaline surge is triggered when enemy is close or when enemy is attacking player from afar, so it works separately from the notification, it is accompanied by sound and visual effect);
  • You choose version by tweaking 6thSenseOn variable in ring settings (value from 0 to 2, 0 = no 6th sense active);
  • 6th sense automatically turns off adrenaline surge when no one is attacking the player. In such case automatic adrenaline surge is immediately possible;
  • 6thSenseAdrenalineInterval (value from 0 to 360) value determines how much time must pass before 6th sense can trigger adrenaline surge again. You can change the variable in ring settings;
  • SixthSenseInterval (value 5-100) value determines frequency of spamming the message. You can change the variable in ring settings;
  • SixthSenseChance (value 1-100) value determines chance to detect the threat. You can change the variable in ring settings;
  • Be advised! 6th sense requires a script to be run on many loaded actors and can potentially affect the speed of the game;

Heat sense

  • The mod will automatically add Heat sense ability to the player that has waterbreathing constant effect;
  • Heat sense detects living creatures and constructs which skins currently emits heat radiation. That means you cannot detect creatures that are swimming or are wet because its raining/snowing;
  • You can detect if the rain/snow falls on current actor, actors beneath a roof will not be detected as wet;
  • You cannot detect living creatures through walls;
  • Actors will gain or loose heat depending on cicrumstances and the change of temperature will be visible. For example actor's heat signature after death will become less and less visible until it disapears completely. The same is with actors when its rainng. When swimming actor looses heat instantly. Actors that start to gain heat will become visible after some time, but again it will be a gradual process;
  • If you press "=" button you turn on heat sense. If you press "=" button again you turn off heat sense. The key can be customised in the ring menu. You can also turn on or off the ability by choosing appropriate power in magic menu and pressing the power button;
  • Even when you have not activated the heat sense there are spells and scripts that are constantly running. In order to stop them you must turn off heat&rain tracking scripts via ring menu;
  • Be advised! Heat sense is extremly complicated and requires many spells and magic effects to be run on any loaded actor and can potentially affect the speed of the game;
  • you can turn on/off heat sense image space modifier (the blue screen effect) via the ring menu;
  • heat vision deactivates night eye and passive night eye if heat sense image space modifier is turned on;
  • some actors (argonians, argonian vampires and terminators) you can sense you as well even while you dont have the heat sense lesser powers
  • you can craft a heat camouflage potion at cooking pot (death bell + nightshade). The potion will completly hide your heat for some time.

Enhanced hearing

  • it detects moving actors even through walls
  • in addition it makes you hear the sounds better (by default volume of sounds is reduced by 30% and once the lesser power is activated the sound level is restored to default) - this is optional and can be turned off via the ring menu

Minions system

Main part


  • You can issue simple commands to above wolves/werewolves and any creature you summoned in vanilla way either with vanilla or custom spells as well as to followers/hirelings/follower dogs both custom and vanilla (minions) no matter what race you are or whether you transformed into alpha werewolf. The commands can be added or removed via ring menu. Up to 25 (sic!) minions supported (but havent tested it). The mod detects them automatically. You dont have to be alpha werewolf to use this
  • The minions will hear your commands if distance is less than 4000, except for follow, where the distance is 8000 (150 is activation distance)
  • Follow me / all follow me - minions will follow you, default key: ",", when you order a minion to retreat it will disengage from combat until it reaches you
  • Wait / all wait - minions will stop where they are, default key: "."
  • Wait there / all wait there - minions will stop where you point, default key: ";", just like in case of forward command you cansee where the actor will go. The marker is optional and can be turned off in the ring menu
  • Forward / all forward - minions will go where you point them and will return after few seconds unless they find enemy, default key: "/" - advanced minion are put into hyper favor mode (see below)
  • While you lock on specific target (using lock on target lesser power or pressing LockOnTargetButton - "'" and looking in the direction of some actor, note that it will be highlighted and some info about it will be provided via notification) and give some minion/minions a forward command, it/they will attack the target, actors hostile to the player and actor nearest to the player have the highest priority. When you press the lock on target button again you switch to another target. In order to turn off lock on target you must loose the eye contact with it or kill it. Attack command for minions (followers) takes into account their morality, if a minion has morality > 0 and is ordered to attack a target not hostile to the player the minion will not obey and its relationship with the player will deteriorate
  • If you issue command to single minion only the minion that is following you will obey the command unless the commmand you are giving is follow me
  • You issue the commands via shout button, but you can also issue follow me, wait and forward commands via buttons you define in the ring menu. In werewolf form you can issue all follow me and all forward commands using the shout (see above)
  • you can heal advanced minions (including followers but not constructs) if it is damaged and you give it healing potion or even cure poison (if it is poisoned and you give it cure poison potion) - not undead or dwarven constructs
  • advanced minions can use poisons to enhance their equipped weapons

Bugs/issues

  • Occasionally the smell ability can describe NPC as FOXRACE when its a humanoid. This is a Skyrim glitch not caused by the mod. This problem should be eliminated now, if not please notify me
  • Sometimes the 6th sense will trigger adrenaline surge even when hostile actor is not attacking you but someone else. This is because it is enough for the actor to spot you to be technically in combat with you. I tried to limit this as much as I can, this will happen only when target can see you and is turned in your more or less exact direction. I dont know how to fix it completely but it should happen very rare. Sometimes 6th sense can trigger adrenaline surge too late for you when hostile actor is using ranged attack and is at medium distance
  • Some quest stages or fights can require you to cast no spells. This is a problem, because although you cant see this the mod constantly causes the player to cast spells via scripts. This is so called brawl bug. In such case you have 3 options: 1) turn off the mod for appropriate time, 2) turn off for appropriate time all passive abilities (night eye, smell, 6th sense), wind script and detection of player by actors and minion script or 3) download and turn on Brawl Bug Plugin (see Recommended mods section above). Its quite easy now to turn off and on all abilities/scripts of the mod. You can do this in ring menu in maintenance section. Just remember to wait for the first punch. Currently I recommend to download Brawl Bugs Patch - Plugins - Modder Resource
  • Minion system now covers also undead actors, but raise dead will not follow your commands due to the issues with thier AI. Their AI is hidden somewhere in the Skyrim engine and to my knowledge no one was able to overwrite/disable it so far. Only summoned actors will follow your commands. I have implemented a special scripted raise dead slave spell that can raise dead actors and allows you to command them via the Minion system;
  • Occasionally you can get an eye glitch. Its a Skyrim glitch and not the fault of the mod. It can be removed by typing in "player.addspell 000f71d1" followed by "player.removespell 000f71d1" in the console;
  • Some people have experienced keybind saving problem. I was told that it seems to be effected by the position in the load order and that the position of my mod cannot exceed position 7F Hex (127 Dec). I havent tested it myself since I donot use such amount of mods myself;
  • Some have experienced that the mod is not working (nothing worked and they even didnt have the ring) or that the smell in not working (no name, just brackets). The solution is restart my mod via the ring menu or to deactivate and activate my mod several times in mod manager (first turn it off via the ring menu) or if thats not working change load order. Alternativelly try this: http://forums.nexusmods.com/index.php?/topic/963218-how-would-one-go-about-reducing-papyrus-based-lag/ The problem with smell seems to be fixed in ver. 2.6;
  • Dont loot any container while in any of my beast forms. You shouldnt be able to do it in the first place but in case you can dont do it or you will loose that object forever. This mechanism was implemented to avoid exploits concerning feeding. In any beast form you are not supposed to pick anything. All you can in a beast form is to feed on something. From ver. 2.8 you loose only what you can eat;
  • Some ENB mods can prevent nightvision effects from working. This is a problem that effects vanilla nightvision as well as vision effects from mods such as this. If you are using an ENB configuration, you must ensure that UseOriginalPostProcessing" is set to `1 in your enbseries.ini" file;
  • From time to time smell tracking is turned off but the visual effect persists. I dont know why. I tried to fix it but I failed. If something like that happens you should start tracking again and then deactivate the tracking. This should fix it;
  • Wispmother Smoke glitch - it appears very rarely, its a Skyrim glitch, to fix read this: http://forums.nexusmods.com/index.php?/topic/768741-wispmother-fx-glitch-fix/?p=6985690;
  • Raise dead NPCs (humanoids) dissapear when left alone in different world space for too long but only NPCs from cities/villages (both guards and civilians), they can be recalled via the ring menu - bandits, hostiles from dungeons are not affected by the issue, this has propably something to do with vanilla Skyrim AI of the actors.
